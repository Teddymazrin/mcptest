# Partner Earned Credit (PEC)

PEC is the mechanism that lets a CSP partner earn credit toward Microsoft
Partner incentives for the Azure consumption they help support.

Key points:
- PEC accrues when the partner has an active delegated relationship (GDAP or Lighthouse) with the appropriate roles during the usage period.
- Losing the delegation, even temporarily, can interrupt PEC accrual for that period.
- PEC is separate from billing; it does not change what the customer pays.
- Partners can check PEC status in Partner Center under the customer's workspace.
